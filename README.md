<h1 align="center">☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard</h1>

<p align="center">
  <b>Interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard using Excel</b>
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>

<p>
You have recently become a franchise owner at <b>Maven Roasters</b>, 
a coffee shop chain with three locations in New York City.
</p>

<p>
To better understand customer purchase behavior and streamline operations, 
transactional sales data from <b>January to June 2023</b> was collected and analyzed.
</p>

<p>
The goal of this project was to transform raw transactional data into a 
<b>dynamic and interactive dashboard</b> that helps franchise owners identify:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Sales trends</li>
  <li>Customer purchasing patterns</li>
  <li>Revenue opportunities</li>
  <li>Operational insights</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Project Objectives</h2>

<ol>
  <li>Profile and prepare raw data for analysis</li>
  <li>Explore the dataset using Excel PivotTables</li>
  <li>Build a fully dynamic dashboard to visualize patterns and trends</li>
</ol>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Dashboard Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>📈 Monthly Revenue Trends (Jan – Jun 2023)</li>
  <li>🏪 Location-wise Sales Analysis</li>
  <li>☕ Top Selling Products</li>
  <li>⏰ Peak Sales Hours</li>
  <li>📅 Sales by Day of Week</li>
  <li>💰 Total Revenue & KPI Metrics</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🛠 Tools & Technologies Used</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Microsoft Excel</li>
  <li>Pivot Tables</li>
  <li>Data Cleaning & Transformation</li>
  <li>Interactive Dashboard Design</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📂 Dataset Information</h2>

<p><b>Data Source:</b> Coffee Shop Sales Data (Jan–Jun 2023)</p>

<p>
The dataset includes:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Transaction ID</li>
  <li>Transaction Date & Time</li>
  <li>Store Location</li>
  <li>Product Category</li>
  <li>Product Name</li>
  <li>Quantity Sold</li>
  <li>Unit Price</li>
  <li>Total Sales</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📈 Key Insights Generated</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Identified peak revenue months</li>
  <li>Discovered top-performing store locations</li>
  <li>Analyzed busiest hours of the day</li>
  <li>Determined best-selling product categories</li>
  <li>Identified opportunities for operational optimization</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🚀 Business Impact</h2>

<p>
This dashboard enables franchise owners to:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Make data-driven inventory decisions</li>
  <li>Optimize staffing during peak hours</li>
  <li>Identify underperforming products</li>
  <li>Increase overall revenue efficiency</li>
</ul>
